Hello Webelos and Arrow of Light Den/Pack Leaders and Parents

Fourth and fifth grade boys and girls are invited to our ghoulishly fun Halloween Orienteering Event at St. Edwards State Park (note location has changed) on Monday, October 18th from 6:30-8:15pm. Model after Cascade Orienteering Club's annual Vampire-O, this is a giant field game, where participants need to find as many checkpoints as possible without being bitten by the vampires that roam the land. It's a combo of Treasure Quest and a Game of Tag or Capture The Flag. We will form groups of 2 or 3 and the game will be about 45 minutes.

Bring a flashlight or headlamp, compass (if you have one) and a whistle. Dress appropriately for the weather. There will be a photo booth and flashing trinkets. Costume is optional.

Check-in is 6:30pm at the picnic tables behind St. Edwards seminary's gymnasium. There is no parking at St. Edwards after dusk. Please park vehicles at Arrowhead Elementary and walk to the park. We will walk back together as a group to Arrowhead Elementary after the event.

RSVP to t570webelos@outlook.com by Sunday, October 17, 2021.

Don't miss this awesome event! We hope to see you Monday evening!!

Camp Pigott
November 5 - 6, 2022

Our Invitation

The Boy Troop 570 and the Girl Troop 570 of Kirkland are hosting a one-night car camping trip at Camp Pigott on November 5 and 6.

All Webelos and Arrow of Light Scouts are invited to come along with a parent and join us for one of our more exciting trips of the year!

Food

Bring your own "sack" lunch for Saturday, Sunday breakfast and any snacks that you might want. We will have hot tea and coffee available at all times for the adults. Please tell us about any allergies or dietary restrictions when you RSVP.

Everyone should have extra food for themselves in case of emergency (extra food is one of the 10 essentials).

Gear to Bring

Bring your Webelos handbook as you will have the chance to work on some activity badge requirements.

Prepare as you would for outdoor overnight Fall outing. The standard BSA Webelos Scout Overnight Checklist in the Cub Scout Leaders Book (page 34-50) is a good one to use. You can also download the checklist from scouting.org.

Bring a tent if you have one or let us know if you want to borrow a tent when you RSVP.

Coordinate whatever group equipment you might need including but not limited to: Tents with poles, stakes, ground cloths and lines, dining fly (in case of liquid sunshine), cooking/cleaning kits, stoves. If you feel you are lacking any other gear, please contact us as our Troop probably has it and would be happy to let you borrow it for the weekend.

You are encouraged to put together a skit, song or run-on for the Saturday evening campfire program. Make sure you bring any props you might need for it.

Fee

The fee for the campout is $5 per person.

Activities

We have many activities planned for the weekend. The Scouts will teach the Webelos skills like

  • fire building
  • knife & axe safety
  • knots & lashings
  • orienteering and more

On Saturday evening, Troop 570 will cook dinner for you. Afterwards, there is going to be an evening campfire program with skits, songs and stories. Webelos are encouraged to participate by adding their own skits, songs or run-ons.

Transportation Details

We will meet at 8:30 am on Saturday morning, November 5th at the north end of the` Kingsgate Park and Ride parking lot and caravan to Camp Pigott. We will be returning around 2:00 pm on Sunday afternoon, October 10th.

Troop 570 can help you unload your gear from the Camp Pigott parking lot to the camping area.

Covid-19 Safety

Troop 570 will observe pandemic safety protocols as determined by the Boy Scouts of America national organization as well as the Chief Seattle Council. All participants must be either vaccinated or had a covid test 72 hours before the event. Masks may be required and any participants are welcome to wear a mask if they feel it is appropriate for them.

Questions

If you have any questions and/or plan on attending, please contact either your Den Leader or the Troop 570 Webelos Liaison at t570webelos@outlook.com.

Please RSVP – Please RSVP by Sunday evening, October 23rd to our Webelos Liason at t570webelos@outlook.com. Let our liason know how many total people you will be bringing up, Webelos and adults. There is limited space and we need to make sure everyone is accommodated.
